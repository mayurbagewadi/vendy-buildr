## What is a WhatsApp Store?

A WhatsApp store is an online store that integrates directly with WhatsApp Business. When a customer visits your store and places an order, you receive an instant WhatsApp notification with the full order details — customer name, items, quantity, address, and payment status.

Customers can also reach you directly via WhatsApp with questions, without ever leaving your store page.

This is different from WhatsApp Business catalog alone, which only displays products but has no checkout, no payment processing, and no order management.

A proper WhatsApp ecommerce setup gives you:
- A professional product catalogue with photos, prices, and descriptions
- A working checkout with UPI, Razorpay, COD, and other Indian payment options
- Automatic WhatsApp order notifications when a customer places an order
- Customer chat via WhatsApp directly from your store
- Order history and inventory management

## How to Receive Orders on WhatsApp India — The Right Way

There are two ways to receive orders on WhatsApp as an Indian seller:

**Method 1 — WhatsApp-only (messy)**
Customers message you product photos from your status or catalog. You manually note the order. You share your UPI QR code. You wait for payment screenshot. You confirm and pack. Repeat 50 times a day.

This works at 5 orders per day. At 30 orders per day, you are drowning in unread messages and missed orders.

**Method 2 — WhatsApp + Online Store (organised)**
Customers browse your store link, add items to cart, checkout and pay online. You receive one clean WhatsApp notification per order: customer name, items, address, payment status. You pack and ship. Done.

At 100 orders per day, this is still manageable. Method 1 is not.
