## What is Dropshipping and How Does It Work in India?

### The Basic Model

1. You build an online store and list products from a supplier
2. A customer visits your store and places an order, paying you
3. You forward the order to your supplier
4. The supplier ships the product directly to your customer under your brand name
5. You keep the margin (your selling price minus supplier cost minus shipping)

### Why Dropshipping Works in India

India has advantages other markets do not:
- Large domestic supplier base (wholesalers in Surat, Delhi, Mumbai, Jaipur)
- Growing online buyer base (800 million+ internet users)
- WhatsApp for direct customer marketing (free, high open rates)
- COD as a widespread payment acceptance method
- Shiprocket and other aggregators for affordable domestic shipping

### Step 1: Choose Your Niche

Successful dropshipping in India happens in niches where:
- There is demand but limited quality options online
- You can source reliably from Indian wholesalers
- The product is not easily found at the same price on Meesho or Amazon

**High-potential niches for Indian dropshipping in 2026:**

### Step 2: Find Indian Dropshipping Suppliers

Unlike Western dropshipping that relies on AliExpress, Indian dropshipping works best with domestic suppliers:

**Where to find suppliers:**
- **IndiaMart** (indiamart.com) — India's largest B2B marketplace. Contact wholesalers directly, negotiate dropshipping arrangements.
- **GlowRoad** — specifically designed for dropshipping in India
- **Meesho Supplier Panel** — for reselling Meesho products
- **Local wholesale markets** — Surat (textiles), Jaipur (jewellery, handicrafts), Delhi (fashion accessories), Mumbai (beauty products)

**What to confirm with suppliers:**
- Do they ship directly to customers? (dropshipping arrangement)
- What is their minimum order? (should be 1 for dropshipping)

## Dropshipping India — Managing Returns and COD Issues

Returns and COD rejections are the biggest operational challenge in Indian dropshipping. Here is how to minimise them:

### Reducing COD Returns

- **Confirm COD orders via WhatsApp** before dispatching. A quick "Your order #123 for [product] is ready to ship. Shall I confirm?" reduces fake orders significantly.
- **Prepaid discount:** Offer ₹20–30 off for online payment vs COD. Shifts some buyers to prepaid.
- **Clear product photos and descriptions:** Most returns happen because the product did not match expectations. Better photos reduce this.

### Handling Supplier Returns

Agree on return policy with supplier upfront:
- What is the return window? (usually 7 days)
- Who bears shipping cost for returns?
- Is replacement or refund provided?
- What is the process for damaged items in transit?

## Dropshipping India — Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is dropshipping legal in India?**

Yes. Dropshipping is completely legal in India. You are a reseller purchasing from a supplier and selling to a customer — this is standard retail commerce. You need a GST registration once your turnover crosses ₹40 lakh, but there is no special licence required for dropshipping.

**Q: How much can you earn from dropshipping in India?**

Earnings depend on niche, marketing effort, and order volume. Typical Indian dropshippers earn ₹10,000–₹50,000/month in their first year with consistent effort. Established dropshipping businesses in India with a strong niche and marketing can earn ₹1,00,000–₹5,00,000/month.

**Q: How do I handle COD returns in my dropshipping business?**

Confirm all COD orders via WhatsApp before shipping. Set a 7-day return policy and agree with your supplier on who handles returns. Keep a small reserve fund (5–10% of revenue) to handle return costs without affecting cash flow.
