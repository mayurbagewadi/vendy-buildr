# Is Wix Really Free Forever? The Honest Truth (+ Free Alternative for India)

You signed up for Wix's free plan, picked a beautiful template, spent two hours arranging your product photos — and then you tried to add a "Buy Now" button. That is when you hit the paywall.

This is the experience of thousands of Indian entrepreneurs every month. Wix markets itself aggressively as a free website builder, and in one narrow sense it is. But "free website" and "free online store" are two completely different things, and Wix does not make that distinction obvious until after you have already invested time building.

This article gives you the honest, factual breakdown of what Wix's free plan actually includes, what it locks away, how much the paid plans cost in India, and whether there is a genuinely free ecommerce option for Indian sellers who need to start selling without a monthly bill.

---

## What Wix's Free Plan Actually Includes

Wix's free plan is a real, functional plan — it does not expire, and Wix does not delete your site after a trial period. That part of the promise is true.

Here is what you actually get on the free plan:

- **A Wix-branded subdomain**: Your site URL will be something like `username.wixsite.com/yoursite` — you cannot connect a custom domain (like `yourstore.in`) without paying.
- **Wix banner ads on your site**: Every page of your free Wix site displays Wix's own advertising. Visitors see "This site was created with Wix.com" — this is not subtle, and it is not optional.
- **500 MB storage**: Enough for a basic informational site. For a product catalogue with multiple images per product, you will hit this limit quickly.
- **Basic website pages**: You can build an About page, a Contact page, a Blog, and informational pages with images and text.
- **Wix's drag-and-drop editor**: The builder itself is fully available. This is genuinely good — Wix's editor is one of the best in the market for design flexibility.

That is the full list. The free plan is a website builder, not a store builder.

---

## What Wix Does NOT Give You for Free

This is the section Wix's marketing glosses over. The following features are completely unavailable on the free plan:

- **Payment processing**: You cannot accept any form of payment — no UPI, no cards, no net banking, no Cash on Delivery. There is no "Buy" button on a free Wix site.
- **Wix ecommerce features**: The Wix ecommerce module (product listings, cart, checkout, order management) requires a paid Business plan. It is not available on the free plan at any level.
- **Custom domain**: Your free site lives on Wix's subdomain. Connecting your own `.in` or `.com` domain costs extra.
- **Remove Wix ads**: The Wix branding on your site can only be removed on paid plans.
- **Selling digital products or services**: Bookings, downloads, subscriptions — all paywalled.
- **Wix Stores app**: The full store management system is locked to Business plans.
- **Abandoned cart recovery**: Paid plan only.
- **Discount codes and coupons**: Paid plan only.
- **Multichannel selling** (Instagram, Facebook shop): Paid plan only.
- **Priority customer support**: Free plan users get basic community support only.
- **Storage beyond 500 MB**: Any meaningful product catalogue needs a paid plan.
- **Bandwidth beyond 500 MB/month**: A site that gets real traffic can exhaust this in days.

The bottom line: the Wix free plan is a personal website or portfolio builder. It was never designed to run an online store.

## Wix Pricing in India

If you want to sell on Wix, here is what you will pay. Wix pricing in India is charged in INR and changes periodically — the figures below reflect typical pricing as of early 2026. Always verify on Wix's official site before deciding.

### Wix Business Plans (required for ecommerce)

| Plan | Monthly Price (INR, billed annually) | What It Unlocks |
|---|---|---|
| Business Basic | ~₹999/month | Accept payments, Wix ecommerce, 20 GB storage, remove Wix ads, custom domain |
| Business Unlimited | ~₹1,699/month | Everything above + 35 GB storage, subscription products, marketplace apps |
| Business VIP | ~₹2,599/month | Everything above + 50 GB storage, priority customer support, loyalty program |

**Key observations for Indian sellers:**

- Even the entry Business Basic plan at ~₹999/month is ₹11,988/year. For a seller doing ₹20,000–30,000/month in revenue, this is a significant fixed cost.
- The Business Basic plan includes payment processing, but you still need to connect a payment gateway separately. In India, that means Razorpay or PayU — and those gateways charge their own per-transaction fees on top of Wix's plan cost.
- There is no free tier that allows even a single transaction. Zero.
- Plans are billed annually to get the advertised monthly rate. Paying month-to-month costs significantly more.

There is also the Wix "Core" plan (~₹599/month) that removes ads and allows a custom domain — but it still does not include ecommerce features. You cannot sell on it.

## So, Is Wix Free Forever?

Yes — and no. Here is the direct answer:

**Wix's free plan does exist permanently.** Your site will not be taken down. You will not be automatically charged after a trial period. In that sense, yes, Wix is free forever.

**But you cannot sell a single product on the free plan.** No payments, no cart, no checkout, no order management. If your goal is to run an online store, the Wix free plan is completely non-functional for that purpose. You will need a Business plan starting at approximately ₹999/month to process even one order.

The "free forever" claim is technically accurate for informational websites. For ecommerce, it is misleading. Any entrepreneur in India who signed up for Wix's free plan expecting to sell products has discovered this the hard way.
