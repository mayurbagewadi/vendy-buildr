## Why Indian Sellers Are Leaving Shopify

Shopify was built for North American and European sellers. When Indian sellers use it, they immediately run into problems:

**1. UPI is not built in.** You need a third-party payment gateway integration (Razorpay or PayU) which adds complexity and sometimes extra fees.

**2. Cash on delivery is clunky.** COD accounts for 40–65% of orders in Tier 2 and Tier 3 Indian cities. Shopify's COD setup requires manual configuration.

**3. WhatsApp ordering is not supported.** Indian customers expect to place orders and ask questions via WhatsApp. Shopify has no native WhatsApp integration.

**4. Shiprocket is not built in.** You need a paid Shopify app to connect with Indian shipping aggregators.

**5. The price is in USD.** Shopify charges in US dollars, which means the ₹ equivalent fluctuates and is always expensive.

**6. Transaction fees on top of subscription.** The basic plan charges 2% per transaction unless you use Shopify Payments — which is not available in India.
