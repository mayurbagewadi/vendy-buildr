A no-code ecommerce website builder is a platform that lets you create a complete online store without any programming knowledge. Instead of writing code, you:

The platform handles everything technical behind the scenes — the website code, hosting, security (SSL), mobile responsiveness, and payment processing.

## What to Look for in a No-Code Ecommerce Builder for India

Not all no-code builders are equal. For Indian sellers specifically, check for:

**1. UPI and COD support built in**
UPI is India's payment backbone. COD accounts for 40–60% of orders in smaller cities. Both must be available without plugins.

**2. WhatsApp integration**
Indian buyers communicate via WhatsApp. Your store must send order notifications to WhatsApp and allow customers to reach you there.

**3. AI store designer**
A good no-code builder in 2026 should include an AI designer that automatically builds your store's visual design — colours, layout, and styling — based on your preferences. No design skills required.

**4. Mobile-first design**
85%+ of Indian internet users access the web via mobile. Your store must be mobile-optimised automatically, not as an afterthought.

**5. Free plan available**
You should not pay before making your first sale. A genuine free plan (not a 14-day trial) lets you validate your product without financial risk.

**6. Shiprocket or shipping integration**
Indian shipping logistics run through aggregators like Shiprocket. Built-in integration saves hours of manual work.
