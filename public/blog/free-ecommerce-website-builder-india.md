## What Makes a Free Ecommerce Website Builder Actually Free?

Before comparing platforms, understand what "free" means in the ecommerce context. There are three types of "free":

**1. Genuinely free** — you can build a store, list products, accept payments, and receive orders without paying anything. You keep the free plan as long as you want.

**2. Free trial** — free for 14–30 days, then you must pay. Shopify and BigCommerce use this model.

**3. Crippled free tier** — technically free but blocks payment processing, limits products to 5, or adds the platform's branding all over your store. Wix uses this model for ecommerce.

For Indian sellers starting out, only genuinely free works. You need to make your first sales before you can afford software costs.
