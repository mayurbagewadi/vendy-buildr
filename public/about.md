# About DigitalDukandar

> DigitalDukandar is a free ecommerce website builder built specifically for Indian entrepreneurs, small businesses, resellers, and content creators.

## What Is DigitalDukandar?

DigitalDukandar is an Indian multi-tenant ecommerce platform that lets anyone create a professional online store in minutes — no coding, no technical skills, no monthly subscription required.

Every store gets:
- Its own subdomain (yourstore.digitaldukandar.in) or custom domain
- A complete product catalogue with cart and checkout
- UPI, card, and net banking payments via Razorpay
- Cash on Delivery (COD) support
- WhatsApp order notifications
- Shiprocket shipping integration across India
- SEO-optimised storefront with meta tags and structured data
- AI-powered store designer

---

## Who Is It For?

DigitalDukandar is built for:

- **Instagram and WhatsApp sellers** — move from DM-based selling to a proper online store with one link
- **Small business owners** — boutiques, grocery stores, local shops going digital
- **Resellers and dropshippers** — list products, collect payments, and ship with Shiprocket
- **Handmade and artisan sellers** — jewellery, handicrafts, home decor, food products
- **Influencers and content creators** — sell merchandise or branded products to your audience
- **Tier 2 and Tier 3 city entrepreneurs** — COD support for customers who prefer cash payment
- **Anyone who wants to start selling online** without hiring a developer or paying monthly fees

---

## Why DigitalDukandar?

Most ecommerce platforms were built for Western markets. They charge in dollars, default to Stripe and PayPal, and treat COD and UPI as afterthoughts. Indian sellers end up paying ₹2,000–₹5,000/month for a platform that still requires third-party plugins for basic Indian payment and shipping workflows.

DigitalDukandar was built from the ground up for India:

- **UPI first** — Razorpay integration handles UPI, PhonePe, Google Pay, cards, net banking
- **COD built in** — cash on delivery works out of the box, no extra setup
- **WhatsApp native** — orders and inquiries go directly to your WhatsApp
- **Shiprocket integration** — book pickups, generate labels, track deliveries across 27,000+ pin codes
- **Free forever** — the core selling platform is permanently free with 0% transaction fees
- **AI Store Designer** — describe the look you want; the AI redesigns your store visually

## Platform Details

- **Headquarters:** India
- **Language:** English (en-IN)
- **Currency:** INR (Indian Rupee)
- **Payment gateway:** Razorpay (UPI, cards, net banking, COD)
- **Shipping:** Shiprocket (Delhivery, Blue Dart, Ekart, DTDC, and more)
- **Technology:** React, TypeScript, Supabase (hosted on Indian servers)
- **Routing:** Subdomain (storename.digitaldukandar.in) and custom domain support
- **Transaction fee:** 0% on all plans

## The Business Development Manager (BDM) / Affiliate Program

DigitalDukandar runs a referral program called the BDM Program. Business Development Managers (BDMs) earn commissions by referring new sellers to the platform. When a referred seller subscribes to a paid plan, the BDM earns a commission.

Ideal for: freelancers, digital marketers, business consultants, and anyone with a network of small business owners.
