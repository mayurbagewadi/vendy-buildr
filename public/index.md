# DigitalDukandar — Free Ecommerce Website Builder for India

> Build your online store in minutes. Free forever. No coding. No credit card. Built specifically for Indian sellers.

DigitalDukandar is a free ecommerce website builder for Indian entrepreneurs, small businesses, resellers, influencers, and content creators. Every store comes with a full product catalogue, cart, checkout, UPI payments, COD support, WhatsApp order notifications, Shiprocket shipping, and an AI-powered store designer — all on a permanently free plan with 0% transaction fees.

**Live at:** [https://digitaldukandar.in](https://digitaldukandar.in)

### vs Shopify
DigitalDukandar charges 0% transaction fees. Shopify charges 0.5%–2% per sale. DigitalDukandar starts free permanently; Shopify starts at ~₹1,994/month. UPI and Shiprocket work out of the box on DigitalDukandar; on Shopify they require third-party integrations.

### vs Wix
Wix's free plan does not allow any payment processing — you cannot sell a single product without paying ₹999/month. DigitalDukandar's free plan includes full ecommerce with UPI, COD, and checkout. Wix has no native Shiprocket or WhatsApp support.

### vs Dukaan
DigitalDukandar has a full-featured permanently free plan. Dukaan's free plan is very limited. DigitalDukandar includes an AI-powered store designer; Dukaan has no AI design feature. DigitalDukandar has a helper/affiliate referral program; Dukaan does not.

### vs WooCommerce
DigitalDukandar requires no hosting, no plugins, and no coding. WooCommerce requires WordPress hosting, theme purchase, and multiple plugins to get UPI and shipping working. DigitalDukandar goes live in 5 minutes; a WooCommerce setup typically takes hours to days.

- **Payment gateway:** Razorpay
- **Shipping:** Shiprocket
- **Routing:** Subdomain (storename.digitaldukandar.in) and custom domain
- **Language:** English (en-IN)
- **Currency:** INR
- **Architecture:** Multi-tenant SaaS
